Philippine Forestry Education Network, Inc.


The Philippine Forestry Education Network, Inc. (PFEN) is an accredited non-profit and non-stock organization of Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) with registration No. CN200415860. It was organized in February 2004 and is based at the College of Forestry and Natural Resources, University of the Philippines Los Ba√±os. Originally, PFEN consisted of 18 different State Colleges and Universities (SUCs) that offer forestry in the country and by 2015, member institutions increased to 30. The Network conducts national conference and general assembly meeting every two years in relevance to the current issues and challenges in the field of forestry, natural resources and environment.

Vision:

An effective, dynamic, sustainable and participatory national network of forestry colleges, schools and institutions committed to the advancement of forestry education in the Philippines.

Mission:

To strengthen and enhance the participation of Philippine forestry schools in the development of forestry education through;

  • Promotion and maintenance of close working relationships among the members and other national and international agencies engaged in forestry;
  • Mutual consultation and formulation of plans, policies and programs that will advance the welfare of the member institutions;
  • Implementation of collaborative undertakings such as research, extension and development projects, joint seminars and other related activities that will promote forestry education in the country; and
  • Exploration of ways by which member institutions can help one another and contribute more effectively to the socio-economic uplift of the Filipino people
